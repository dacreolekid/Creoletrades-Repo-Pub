What is This Course?

Dollar Cost Averaging (DCA) is not some get-rich-quick scheme. It's the cornerstone of professional investing. Whether you're building an index fund portfolio, accumulating individual stocks, or stacking crypto—DCA removes the noise, removes the emotion, and removes the pressure of timing the market perfectly.

This course teaches you exactly how DCA works, why it works, and how to implement it in your own portfolio. You'll learn the math behind compounding, when to use DCA vs lump sum investing, and advanced strategies like Value Averaging that the professionals use.

By the end, you'll understand the mental framework that separates traders from hoarders—and why DCA is the S in STACK.

What You'll Learn

  • The exact mechanics of DCA and why it beats emotion-based investing
  • The power of compounding with real-world examples and math
  • How to choose assets, set contribution frequency, and determine amounts
  • DCA vs lump sum: when each strategy wins
  • Advanced strategies: Value Averaging, Tactical DCA, multi-asset portfolios
  • Tax optimization and platform selection for automated DCA
  • Real projections: 5-year, 10-year, and 20-year DCA timelines
